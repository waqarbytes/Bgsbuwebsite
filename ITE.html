About the Department

The Department of Information Technology and Engineering (ITE) was established in 2007 and is one of the pioneer Departments of School of Engineering. We offer comprehensive B.Tech and Ph.D. programs designed to meet the evolving demands of the technology sector. Our curriculum is regularly updated to reflect current industry trends and future directions, ensuring our students remain at the forefront of technological innovation.
